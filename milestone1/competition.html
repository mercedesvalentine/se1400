Competitive Analysis

What types of content do competitors use on their websites?

The content on the art portfolio websites is mostly focused on showcasing the artist's artwork and introducing themselves.

What types of images are used?

All of the art portfolio websites use images of the art that they have created, and sometimes their website will have a picture of them.

What color schemes are used?

Most of the art portfolio websites use a soft color scheme so that the background doesn't take away from their artwork.

Layout styles and techniques used?

Most of the art portfolio websites use a clean layout with a lot of white space and a simple navigation bar at the top of the website. Some of the websites seem to use javascript or css to create interactive elements with a fun design.

What are some things visually I find appealing?

On one of the websites, I love how the creater used some of her artwork to create buttons/links to different pages on her website. I also love how most of the websites are clean around the artwork so the artwork is the focus and will pop out.

What are some things visually I find unappealing?

Most of the websites are almost completely white, besides the artwork itself, which is kind of boring or uninteresting to look at. I also don't like how some of the websites almost don't have anything to click on or that link to something else in their website.
