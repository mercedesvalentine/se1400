Website Summary

What content will the site contain?

I will talk about and showcase all of my art pieces that I have created.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I will get all of my content from either pictures I have taken of my art pieces or from scaned in pictures.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users should care about the focus of this site because it presents all of the main art that I have created and by looking at each art piece the user will learn a little bit more about me.

Why should a site be built for this?

I believe that a site should be built for this because it may help me have a better chance of getting jobs. It also can help me share my talent and hard work with those around the world.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would love for my users to come to my site and look around at all of my art, and while they are looking at my art I would love for them to hopefully feel something in their heart.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of this site is to share my love for art and my art pieces with the world. This site is to maybe inspire people to create thier own art, or use their talents for good.
