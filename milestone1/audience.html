Audience Analysis

Where does the primary audience live?

The primary audience for this art portfolio website most likely lives in the United States because my website will be in english as default.

What is the age range that will primarily use the site?

The primary audience will most likely be between the ages of 30 and 40. This is because my website will be professional, but fun to see as well.

What will the gender be of the majority of your users?

The majority of most of the users will probably be female because I am a female artist and my website will most likely look pretty feminine. Because of this, my website will be appealing to female gender more than the male gender.

What will be the primary interest of your users?

The primary interest of my users will most likely be the interest of God's creations. This is because many of the artwork that I create is based off of the beautiful masterpieces that God has created.
