Why I Create Art

Art has always been a big part of my life. I have been creating art for as long as I can remember. I would color, or paint, or draw and I have always loved to use my creativity. I began seriously pursuing my passion for art when I was six years old. I started to try and draw relistic pictures on my own by looking at reference picutres. As the years have passed I have gotten better at my artistic skills, and I have continued to develop my unique style by experimenting with different techniques and mediums. I love creating art that has meaning and that gives a positive feeling. I hope you can feel positive feelings as you look at my artwork. Thank you for visiting!
